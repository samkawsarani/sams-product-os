# Company Overview Example

Example of what to include in `knowledge/company-context/company-overview.md` to help AI understand your business context.

---

## Example Content

```markdown
# Company Overview

## Mission
[Your company mission]

## Products
[What you build, who it's for]

## Target Customers
- [Customer segment 1]: [Description]
- [Customer segment 2]: [Description]

## Team Structure
- Product: [Team size, structure]
- Engineering: [Team size, structure]
- Design: [Team size, structure]

## Current Stage
[Startup, growth, enterprise]

## Key Stakeholders
- [Name, role, what they care about]
```

---

## More Detailed Example

```markdown
# Company Overview

## Mission
Make payments invisible for online merchants.

## Products
- **Checkout SDK** — Drop-in payment form for e-commerce platforms
- **Merchant Dashboard** — Self-serve analytics, settlement reports, dispute management
- **API Platform** — RESTful APIs for custom integrations

## Target Customers
- **Mid-market merchants** ($5M-$50M GMV): Need reliability and support
- **Enterprise merchants** ($50M+ GMV): Need customization and dedicated CSM
- **Platform partners**: Embed our checkout in their marketplace

## Team Structure
- Product: 4 PMs, 1 Director
- Engineering: 30 engineers across 5 squads
- Design: 3 product designers, 1 UX researcher

## Current Stage
Series B, 120 employees, post-PMF scaling into enterprise

## Key Stakeholders
- **CTO** — Cares about platform stability and tech debt
- **VP Sales** — Cares about enterprise features and competitive gaps
- **Head of Support** — Cares about merchant self-serve and ticket volume
```

---

## Tips

- Keep it updated as your company evolves
- Focus on what AI needs to understand your business context
- Include stakeholder motivations — helps AI align recommendations

---

**Remember**: This helps AI understand your business so it can give context-aware advice.
